About Us


Bookstop is a one stop site where one can get required information about any book, the books by a particular author, or any other books by that name. It also has a book lending and reading community, inclusive of all bookworms, a user can sign-up, and upon login they can choose whether they ant to lend or read.
Lenders can enter the details to the library and lend their books for reading. This site is for all those bookworms looking for one place to search for a newlook, read or lend without any interruptions. One stop for all those book lovers, BookStop.
